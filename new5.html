          What is a carbon offset?


          A carbon offset is a reduction in emissions of carbon dioxide or other greenhouse gases made in order to compensate for emissions made           elsewhere.Frequently carbon offsetting reduces emissions much faster than you can as an individual/single company. Carbon offsetting projects           help to combat global climate change as well as caring for local communities.
